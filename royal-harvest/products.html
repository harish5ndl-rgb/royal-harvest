Rice Varieties We Export

At Royal Harvest Global Trade, we provide authentic Indian rice varieties celebrated for their rich taste, delicate aroma, and natural nutrition — locally cherished and ready to be discovered by global markets.

BPT-5204 (Samba Masuri)

BPT-5204, popularly known as Samba Masuri, is one of the most widely consumed rice varieties in India. It produces soft, fluffy rice with a pleasant taste, making it ideal for daily meals.

  • Fine Slender Grain
  • Soft & Fluffy Texture
  • Protein: 6.8 g /100g
  • Carbohydrates: 78 g /100g
  • Energy: 345 kcal
  • Glycemic Index: 58-64

NDLR-7 (Nandyal Sona)

NDLR-7 is a fine grain rice variety known for higher yield and excellent cooking quality. It produces soft, fluffy rice that is widely preferred for everyday cooking.

  • Short-Medium Slender Grain
  • Soft & Fluffy Texture
  • Protein: 6.5-7 g /100g
  • Carbohydrates: 78 g /100g
  • Energy: 345 kcal
  • Glycemic Index: 60-66

RNR-15048 (Telangana Sona)

RNR-15048, also known as Telangana Sona, is gaining popularity due to its excellent cooking quality and relatively lower glycemic index.

  • Medium Slender Grain
  • Soft & Light Texture
  • Protein: 6.7 g /100g
  • Carbohydrates: 77-78 g /100g
  • Energy: 345 kcal
  • Glycemic Index: 51-55

Mangoes We Export

Royal Harvest Global Trade offers premium Indian mango varieties such as Alphonso, Totapuri, and the famous Benisha Mango, known for their exceptional sweetness, aroma, and international appeal.

Alphonso Mango

Often called the 'King of Mangoes', Alphonso is prized for its rich, creamy texture and intense sweetness. Grown in the Ratnagiri region, it is exported fresh to markets worldwide.

  • Rich & Creamy Texture
  • High Beta-Carotene
  • Carbohydrates: 15 g/100g
  • Vitamin C: 36% RDA
  • Calories: 60 kcal/100g

Benisha Mango

Benisha Mango is a premium hybrid variety prized for its attractive golden‑yellow skin with a reddish blush, sweet flavor, excellent shelf life, high yield, and strong market demand. Farmers and exporters favour it for its large, uniform fruits and growing popularity.

  • Fiberless, Smooth Flesh
  • Sweet Aromatic Flavor
  • Carbohydrates: 14 g/100g
  • Vitamin C: 35% RDA
  • Potassium: 150 mg/100g

Totapuri Mango

Known for its tangy flavor and distinct beak-like shape, Totapuri is ideal for juices, pulps, and fresh consumption. It has a longer shelf life, making it suitable for export.

  • Tangy & Refreshing
  • Great for Processing
  • Carbohydrates: 13 g/100g
  • Vitamin A: 54% RDA
  • Fiber: 1.6 g/100g
